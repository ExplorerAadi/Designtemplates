Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering

Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering

INTAKE: 60 seats.


Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering (EJ) involves the study of different audio & visual transmitters and receivers that mobile devices use as part of a larger network. Today, when the world has become a global village, technology & communication plays an important role in bringing people together.It also involves studying wireless connections using satellite technology, microwave and fiber optics communication. Engineers in this discipline have opportunities in the fields of TV & radio equipment, networking, defense research, security, Internet, air traffic control, weather forecasting and military applications. All these areas require high-speed, error free and secure transmission of data. The course also includes developments in the field of computer networks and wireless networks.

LABORATORIES: Basic Electronics Lab , Microprocessor Lab , Communication Lab , Digital Electronics Lab.
