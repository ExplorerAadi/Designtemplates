Civil Engineering

INTAKE: 60 seats.


Civil Engineering (CE) s a discipline that deals with the design, construction and maintenance of physically or naturally built environments including bridges, roads, canals,dams and buildings. Traditionally, it is broken into several sub-disciplines including environmental engineering, geo-technical engineering, structural engineering,transportation engineering, municipal or urban engineering, water resource engineering, coastal engineering, surveying and construction engineering. In some firms, entry-level engineers are responsible for monitoring construction in the field, while in other firms, they are expected to do more complex analysis or design work. Senior engineers can get into specialized consulting including forensic engineering.
