Automobile Engineering

INTAKE: 60 seats.


Automobile Engineering (AE) involves the study and application of principles from various branches of engineering such as electrical/electronics, mechanical and computer science. This course will enable students to design, manufacturer and maintain automobiles. The course will include the study of mechanics, IC engines, thermodynamics, kinematics as well as vehicle systems, maintenance of vehicles, vehicle design and other electrical systems related to automobiles. Careers for Automobile Engineers include working in manufacturing units and assembly plants, sales, servicing, claim surveying and vehicle insurance among others.

LABORATORIES: Automobile Lab , Advanced Automobile Lab , Fluid Power Lab , Thermal Lab , Auto Electrical Lab.
