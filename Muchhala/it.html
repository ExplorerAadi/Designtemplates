Information Technology

INTAKE: 30 seats.


Information Technology(IT) is the study, design, development, implementation, support or management of computer based information systems, particularly software applications and computer hardware. IT deals with the use of electronic computers and computer software to convert, store, protect, process, transmit and securely retrieve information. IT professionals perform a variety of duties such as installing applications, designing complex computer networks and creating information databases. It may also include data management, networking, engineering computer hardware, database & software design as well as management and administration of entire systems. Since IT is a vital part of business, the field will continue to grow in the future.
